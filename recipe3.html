APPLESAUCE CAKE

YIELD: 12 servings.
  • 2 cups sifted cake flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon double-acting _or_ 1½ teaspoons cream of tartar baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon soda
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ cup shortening
  • ⅓ cup GRANDMA’S MOLASSES
  • 1 cup unsweetened applesauce
  • 2 eggs

Heat oven to 375° F. (moderate). Sift first seven ingredients into a mixing bowl. Add shortening, molasses, and applesauce. Mix until all flour is dampened. Beat 2 minutes by hand or electric beater (low speed). Add eggs. Beat 2 more minutes. Bake 25 minutes or until done in 2 well-greased, lightly floured, round, 8-inch, layer-cake pans. Cool; frost with MOLASSES MOCHA FROSTING
